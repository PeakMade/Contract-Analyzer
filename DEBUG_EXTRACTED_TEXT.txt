PARTERNSHIP AGREEMENT

This AGREEMENT is made and entered into this 5th day of August 2024, between Hotel Internet Services, LLC (Herein known as HIS), a Delaware corporation, having principal offices at 50 S. Belcher Rd. suite 116 Clearwater FL 33765, AND: Redpoint Connect, LLC (“Partner”), a Delaware corporation having principal offices at 2970 Clairmont Rd #310, Atlanta, GA 30329.

WITNESSETH

WHEREAS, HIS is engaged in providing and/or Internet Systems, BeyondTV VOD, IPTV & Streaming Systems, monitoring, support services and maintenance.

WHEREAS, The Partner has existing relationships and or prospects that have a need for HIS’ products and services and wishes to refer them to HIS.

WHEREAS, The parties agree to perform under the terms and conditions contained herein, for a period commencing on the date above and expiring on such date as the agreement is terminated in accordance with Paragraph six (V1), herein:

NOW THEREFORE, In Consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, HIS and The Partner agree as follows:

1. The services to be rendered hereunder by The Partner include consulting with HIS from time to time, preparing comprehensive selling strategies for the sale of HIS Products and make appointments with business customers to demonstrate the products, assist in preparing sales proposals and purchasing decisions resulting from such events. All sales are to be offered, proposed and made based on HIS published prices.

2. The parties expressly intend and agree that The Partner is an independent contractor, and retains sole and absolute discretion, control and judgement in the manner and means of carrying out his assignments. No representations made by The Partner shall bind or commit HIS in any way, nor shall The Partner infer in any way that he/she is authorized to make any representations on behalf of HIS.

3. During the term of this agreement, HIS shall pay The Partner as described in Schedule “A”. Upon acceptance by HIS of said contract, HIS shall pay The Partner no later than thirty (30) days after the invoice is paid by the property. The Partner acknowledges and agrees that no payment will be paid until HIS receives payment on property invoice.

4. Confidentiality & Non-Compete. The Partner may have access to certain information, the ownership and CONFIDENTIAL status of which is proprietary to HIS including but not limited to trade secrets, business plans, distribution process, R&D, technical information, customer lists, pricing and other information designated by HIS as Confidential. The Partner agrees to exercise the highest degree of care in safeguarding such confidential information against disclosure, loss or theft and takes all necessary steps to maintain such Confidentiality. The parties explicitly intend this provision to survive the termination of the agreement. The Partners Services Information Material and Pricing is the Property of HIS and shall not be shared with any other persons outside of HIS except with written approval of HIS.

5. This agreement shall commence on the date herein and remain in effect unless terminated by either party upon thirty (30) days written notice. Should this agreement be terminated by either party, The Partner shall be entitled to payment for the duration of the term of contract for any customers referred to HIS.

6. Neither this agreement nor the rights, payments or benefits of this agreement shall be assignable (not assignable) by The Partner without the expressed written consent of HIS.

7. In the event of a dispute arising out of this agreement, the parties waive their rights to file lawsuits for trial, and agree that any such disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration, under the rules of the American Arbitration Association and with jurisdiction in Los Angeles, CA. The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover all expenses, fees and costs incurred in the Arbitration.

8. Any provision of the agreement which may be deemed illegal, invalid or unenforceable shall in no way invalidate any other provisions, all of which shall remain in full force and effect.

9. All notices or communications required hereunder shall be in writing, sent postage paid, to the respective party at the address set forth herein.

10. Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other from liabilities, claims, demands, costs, attorney’s fees, judgments or any cause of action arising out of or in connection with negligence or willful misconduct of that party’s employee. Neither party shall be liable for delays in performing, or failure to perform under this agreement due to causes beyond the control of the party including but not limited to acts of God, fires, floods, strikes, war or intervention by any third party or government authority.

Each party hereto warrants that it has full authority to enter into this agreement and that this agreement is not in conflict with any other agreement or contract. This agreement contains the entire agreement between the parties relating to the subject matter herein, and may not be amended except in writing and signed by both parties and their authorized representative.

Partner Agreement ATTACHMENT A – Payments

Payment Terms

1. The Company agrees to pay the Partner $3.00 per bed/unit for any deal that is closed together.

2. Payments will be made every month, until the end of the property’s contract, within thirty (30) days following the closing of a deal and upon receipt of an invoice from the Partner.

3. The Partner shall provide detailed records of the number of beds included in each closed deal.
